My journey in cybersecurity began at the U.S. Naval Academy, where I earned a degree in Cyber Operations—a program recognized as a Center of Academic Excellence in Cyber Operations (CAE-CO). That foundation instilled both technical expertise and a mission-focused approach to protecting critical systems. As a U.S. Navy Lieutenant, I’ve led and supported operations in Cyber Defense and Red Teaming, identifying vulnerabilities, simulating real-world threats, and implementing defensive strategies in high-stakes environments. I see cybersecurity not just as a technical field but as a vital mission: to safeguard the systems, infrastructure, and people that keep our world connected and secure.
